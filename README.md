# Retail-Sales-Dashboard
📊 Retail Sales Dashboard (Excel) | 2022–2024

📌 Project Overview:

This project focuses on building an interactive Retail Sales Dashboard using Microsoft Excel to analyse and visualise sales performance across multiple dimensions such as regions, sales channels, product categories, and time (2022–2024). The goal of this project is to transform raw retail data into meaningful insights that can support business decision-making, identify growth opportunities, and improve overall operational efficiency.

🎯 Objective:

To analyse retail sales data and identify key trends and patterns To evaluate performance across regions, channels, and categories To understand the impact of promotions on sales To provide a clear and interactive dashboard for quick decision-making

📂 Dataset Description:

The dataset includes the following fields: Region (PL-Central, PL-North, PL-South) Channel (Retail, Discount, E-commerce) Year (2022, 2023, 2024) Product Category (Juice, Milk, ReadyMeal, SnackBar, Yogurt) Pack Type (Carton, Multipack, Single) Revenue Units Sold Delivered Quantity Stock Available Price per Unit

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used:

Microsoft Excel Pivot Tables Pivot Charts Slicers for interactivity Data Cleaning & Transformation Dashboard Design & Data Visualization

⚙️ Process & Methodology:

Data Cleaning & Preparation Checked for missing values and inconsistencies Standardised data formats (numbers, currency, units) Ensured proper categorisation of variables
Data Transformation Created calculated fields such as: Total Revenue Total Units Sold Average Price per Unit Aggregated data using Pivot Tables
Dashboard Development Designed KPI cards for quick overview: Total Revenue: ₹19.95M Units Sold: 3.80M Delivered Quantity: 34.21M Stock Available: 30.08M Average Price: ₹5.25 Implemented slicers for: Region Channel Year Category Pack Type
Data Visualisation Monthly Trend Analysis (Line Chart) Category-wise Revenue & Units (Bar Chart) Brand-level Performance (Clustered Bar Chart) Regional Contribution (Pie Chart) Channel-wise Revenue Share (Donut Chart) Promotion vs Non-Promotion Analysis (Column Chart)
🔍 Key Insights:

📈 Seasonality in Sales: Revenue increases steadily during the first half of the year and peaks mid-year, followed by a decline — indicating seasonal demand patterns. 🛍️ Top Performing Categories: Yogurt and SnackBar categories generate higher revenue compared to other categories, making them key contributors to business growth. 🌍 Balanced Regional Performance: Revenue contribution from all regions is almost equal (~33% each), indicating a well-distributed market presence. 🛒 Channel Analysis: Retail and Discount channels contribute slightly more compared to E-commerce, highlighting an opportunity for digital channel expansion. 🎯 Impact of Promotions: Promotional sales consistently outperform non-promotional sales, proving that marketing campaigns significantly influence revenue. 📦 Inventory Observation: Stock availability is relatively high compared to units sold, suggesting potential overstocking or inefficient inventory management.

📊 Business Impact:

This dashboard helps stakeholders: Monitor key performance indicators in real time Identify top-performing products and categories Analyse customer buying patterns and seasonal trends Evaluate effectiveness of promotional strategies Make data-driven decisions for sales and inventory planning

💡 Recommendations:

Focus more on high-performing categories to maximise profitability Optimise inventory management to reduce excess stock Strengthen E-commerce strategy to capture untapped market potential Continue and refine promotion campaigns during peak sales periods Explore region-specific strategies to boost growth further 🚀 Learning Outcomes Through this project, I gained hands-on experience in: Data cleaning and preparation in Excel Building interactive dashboards Using Pivot Tables and slicers effectively Identifying trends and deriving actionable insights Presenting data in a clear and structured way
